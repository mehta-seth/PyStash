Fibonacci Sequence

Fibonacci sequence is such that each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1. .

The Fibonacci series example is 0,1,1,2,3,5,8â€¦

Code:


def fibonacci_series(n):
    a=0
    b=1
    print(a," ",b," ",end=" ")
    for i in range(3,n):
        c=a+b
        print(c," " ,end=" ")
        a=b
        b=c
n=int(input("ENTER THE RANGE OF FIBONACCI SERIES"))
fibonacci_series(n)
